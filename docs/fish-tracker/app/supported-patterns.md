## Supported Log Patterns

- Fish Catches:

```
NICE CATCH! You caught a Fish.
NICE CATCH! You caught a Fish with a length of Xcm. (it just takes fish name)
```

- Augment Fish Catches:

```
NICE CATCH! Your Augments caught a Fish
NICE CATCH! Your Augments caught a Fish with a length of Xcm. (it just takes fish name)
```

- New entry fish catches:

```
NEW ENTRY! You caught a Bronze Fish for the first time.
```
